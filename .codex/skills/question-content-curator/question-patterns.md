# Question Patterns

## Strong Prompt Shapes

- "Que recuerdo de..." for nostalgia.
- "Cual seria tu..." for imagination.
- "Que pequena cosa..." for warmth.
- "Cuando fue la ultima vez..." for specific stories.
- "Que lugar..." for travel and memory.
- "Que opinion cambiaste sobre..." for reflection.
- "Que te da curiosidad de..." for dates and getting to know someone.

## Patterns To Use Sparingly

- "Prefieres X o Y?" works as a warm-up, but can become shallow.
- "Alguna vez..." often becomes yes/no unless followed by "que paso?".
- "Cual es tu favorito..." is easy but repetitive.

## Avoid

- "Confiesa..."
- "Dime algo que nunca le has dicho a nadie..."
- "Que trauma..."
- "Por que eres asi..."
- Forced intimacy, dares, shaming, or interrogation.

## Mobile Length Rule

Prefer one sentence under 120 characters. Allow longer prompts only when the wording earns it.
