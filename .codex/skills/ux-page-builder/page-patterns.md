## Above-The-Fold Rule

The first viewport should answer:

- What is this?
- Is it for me right now?
- What do I do first?
- Can I see or reach useful content immediately?

## Interaction Model Options

- Browse-first: show many items, then let users filter or open one.
- Card-first: show one strong item with next/copy/share controls.
- Category-first: choose a deck, then enter browse or card mode.
- Hybrid: show a featured card and a visible list of indexable items below.

For this project, prefer hybrid on SEO pages and card-first inside focused deck experiences.
